THE PREMISE

Build a deck from
the history of ideas.

Source of Thought turns works—not heroes or monsters—into playable cards. A book, theory, discovery, or philosophy receives a distinct thought profile through ThoughtMap.

Your deck is more than a stack of strong cards. It is a composition of ideas. Their position, affinity, and conceptual distance determine how they act together.

CORE SYSTEMS

Strategy before spectacle.

Every system asks the same question: what changes when thought becomes playable?

01

BATTLE SYSTEM

5 × 5 formation combat

Deploy five cards from a ten-card deck. Front and back rows change attack, defense, and target priority. Adjacent works create resonance bonuses or penalties.

  • 5 vs 5 auto battle
  • Position-based hate and defense
  • Mind / Human / System affinities

02

CARD SYSTEM

One work. One card.

Ten parameters translate each work into combat stats. Generated skills reflect the concepts contained within the source instead of relying on fixed character classes.
