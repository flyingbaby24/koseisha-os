THE PREMISE

Build a deck from
the history of ideas.

Source of Thought turns works—not heroes or monsters—into playable cards. A book, theory, discovery, or philosophy receives a distinct thought profile through ThoughtMap.

Your deck is more than a stack of strong cards. It is a composition of ideas. Their position, affinity, and conceptual distance determine how they act together.

CORE SYSTEMS

Strategy before spectacle.

Every system asks the same question: what changes when thought becomes playable?

01

BATTLE SYSTEM

5 × 5 formation combat

Deploy five cards from a ten-card deck. Front and back rows change attack, defense, and target priority. Adjacent works create resonance bonuses or penalties.

Source of Thought

Development Blog

A technical and design journal documenting the creation of Source of Thought — a strategy card game where literature, philosophy, science, and human thought become playable cards. Rather than recording only what was built, this blog focuses on why each system was designed.

Development Timeline

2026 • Project Start

Why Build a Game from Thought?

The project began with a simple question: "What if ideas themselves could become game pieces?" Instead of collecting monsters or heroes, players collect books, philosophies, scientific discoveries, and historical works. Each work becomes a card with unique parameters, allowing players to construct a deck based on different schools of thought.

Concept Game Design
Architecture

Separating Search from Battle

One of the earliest architectural decisions was splitting the application into four completely independent scenes: Input / Register Search / Collect Battle Preparation Battle This separation keeps responsibilities isolated, making the project easier to maintain while allowing each feature to evolve independently.

Architecture
Battle System

Designing Resonance

Cards don't simply fight. Adjacent cards resonate with each other, creating buffs or penalties based on their conceptual similarity. The battlefield itself becomes a network of interacting ideas, rather than a collection of isolated units.

Gameplay
AI Development

Building with AI

Source of Thought is not generated by AI. Instead, AI serves as an implementation partner. Design decisions, game balance, and architecture remain human-driven, while repetitive coding, refactoring, and iteration are accelerated through AI collaboration.
